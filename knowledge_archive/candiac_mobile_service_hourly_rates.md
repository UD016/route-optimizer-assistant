## Purpose

This document provides the standard hourly billing rates for Mobile Generator Service effective January 1, 2026.

It is intended to help Service Coordinators determine the appropriate labor rate, mileage charge, overtime code, and minimum billing requirements when creating quotations and work orders.

> **Note**
>
> This document is for **internal use only**.
>
> Rates may change without notice.

---

# Effective Date

- Effective: **January 1, 2026**

---

# Standard Business Hours

## Monday to Friday (Working Days)

| Time | Generator Size | Hourly Rate | BMS Rate Type |
|------|----------------|------------:|---------------|
| 08:00 – 17:00 | 0–250 kW | **$233.26/hr** | ISRT – Tier A |
| 08:00 – 17:00 | 251–999 kW (Default) | **$239.80/hr** | ISRT – Tier B |
| 08:00 – 17:00 | 1000 kW and above | **$252.88/hr** | ISRT – Tier C |

# Mileage

Mileage is billed at:

| Charge | Rate |
|---------|-----:|
| Mileage | **$3.25/km** |

Applies at all times.

---

# Travel Expenses

Travel-related expenses required to perform the work are billed at:

**Cost + 15% administrative fee**

Examples include:

- Hotel
- Airfare
- Taxi
- Parking
- Accommodation
- Other approved travel expenses

---

# Overtime Rate Codes

The following rate codes are added at the end of the day when applicable.

| Rate Type | Hourly Rate | BMS Code |
|-----------|------------:|----------|
| OT (Tier B + $23) | **$262.80/hr** | FSOT |
| DT (Tier B + $46) | **$285.80/hr** | FSDT |

---

# Minimum Billing Requirement

A minimum of **4 hours of labor** at the applicable labor rate must be billed whenever a technician is dispatched.

This applies to:

- Scheduled service calls
- Emergency service calls

---

# Coordinator Responsibilities

Before creating a quotation or work order:

- Determine the generator size to select the correct standard labor tier.
- Verify whether after-hours, weekend, or holiday rates apply.
- Apply the correct BMS rate code.
- Apply the correct mileage charge.
- Include applicable travel expenses.
- Verify whether OT or DT rates apply.
- Ensure the minimum 4-hour labor charge is respected for technician dispatches.

---

# Common Mistakes

## Incorrect Generator Tier

Always verify the generator size before selecting the labor rate.

---

## Missing Labor Override

After-hours, weekend, and holiday work requires the appropriate **Labor Override** rate.

---

## Incorrect Overtime Code

Verify whether **FSOT** or **FSDT** should be used.

---

## Minimum Labor Not Applied

Every technician dispatch must include a minimum of **4 billable labor hours** at the applicable rate.
