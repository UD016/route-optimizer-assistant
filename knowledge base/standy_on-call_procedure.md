## Purpose

This procedure explains how the after-hours standby (on-call) process operates, including call routing, responsibilities, compensation, and escalation procedures.

---

## Overview

Outside normal business hours, customer service calls are managed through the company's third-party answering service.

Business hours are:

- Monday to Friday
- 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM

Calls received:

- After 4:00 PM
- Before 7:30 AM
- Weekends
- Statutory holidays

are automatically routed to the **OnCall Centre**, which screens the call and dispatches it to the appropriate on-call employee.

---

## Company On-Call Phone

The employee assigned to standby receives the company on-call phone for the duration of their scheduled rotation.

Responsibilities include:

- Answering after-hours customer calls.
- Determining the nature and urgency of the request.
- Opening emergency work orders when required.
- Coordinating technician dispatches when necessary.
- Following normal emergency dispatch procedures.

---

## OnCall Centre

After-hours calls are handled by:

**OnCall Centre**

Website:

https://oncallcentre.com/contact/

The OnCall Centre is responsible for:

- Receiving after-hours customer calls.
- Identifying the appropriate on-call employee.
- Forwarding calls to the company on-call phone.

---

## Escalation Procedure

If after-hours calls are not being routed correctly:

1. Confirm the issue is not related to the company on-call phone.
2. Contact the OnCall Centre using the contact information available on their website.
3. If the issue cannot be resolved, submit an IT Service Ticket.
4. Request escalation to **Mark Tully** for additional support.

---

## Standby Compensation

### Weekly Standby Premium

The weekly standby premium has been increased from **$200** to **$235**.

The standby premium consists of:

- **$25** for each weekday evening.
- **$55** for each weekend day.
- **$55** for each statutory holiday.

These rates reflect the cost-of-living adjustment between 2021 and 2026.

Standby compensation is reviewed annually based on changes to the cost of living.

---

## Administrative Work Order Compensation

Administrative employees assigned to standby are also compensated for emergency work orders opened during their standby period.

Compensation:

- **$35** for each Work Order (WO) opened while on standby.

During each standby rotation, administrators should:

- Record every emergency Work Order opened.
- Submit the list of Work Orders to management for payroll processing.

---

## Coordinator Responsibilities During Standby

While assigned to standby, coordinators should:

- Carry the company on-call phone at all times.
- Remain available to receive after-hours calls.
- Determine the urgency of customer requests.
- Follow normal dispatch procedures.
- Record all Work Orders opened during standby.
- Report any telephone routing issues immediately.
