## Purpose

This document provides the current service billing rates used by the Candiac and Ottawa branches for both Mobile and In-Shop service.

It is intended to help Service Coordinators determine the correct labor rates, overtime rates, mileage charges, minimum billing requirements, and BMS labor types when preparing quotations and invoices.

>

### Mileage

Mileage is billed at:

| Charge | Rate |
|---------|-----:|
| Mileage | **$3.25/km** |

Applies at all times.

### Travel Expenses

Travel-related expenses required to perform the work are billed at:

**Cost + 15% administrative fee**

Examples include:

- Hotel
- Airfare
- Taxi
- Parking
- Accommodation
- Other approved travel expenses

### Overtime

The following overtime rate codes are added at the end of the day when applicable.

| Rate Type | Hourly Rate | BMS Code |
|-----------|------------:|----------|
| OT | **$262.80/hr** | FSOT |
| DT | **$285.80/hr** | FSDT |

### Minimum Billing

A minimum of **4 hours of labor** at the applicable labor rate must be billed whenever a technician is dispatched.

This applies to:

- Scheduled service calls
- Emergency service calls

# Coordinator Responsibilities

Before preparing quotations or invoices:

- Select the correct branch.
- Determine whether the work is Mobile or In-Shop.
- Verify the generator size or service category.
- Apply the correct BMS labor type.
- Apply overtime where applicable.
- Verify mileage charges.
- Apply the minimum labor charge where required.
- Verify the current rates against the official SharePoint rate folder when needed.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Selecting the incorrect branch rates.
- Selecting the wrong Mobile/In-Shop rate.
- Applying the wrong generator tier.
- Forgetting the minimum four-hour labor charge.
- Forgetting overtime rates.
- Using outdated billing rates.
