## Purpose

This document provides the standard hourly billing rates for Candiac In-Shop Generator Service.

It is intended to help Service Coordinators determine the appropriate labor rate and BMS rate type when creating quotations and work orders for in-shop service.

> **Note**
>
> This document is for **internal use only**.
>
> Rates may change without notice.

# Effective Date

- Effective: **January 1, 2026**

# Standard Business Hours

## Monday to Friday (Working Days)

| Time | Service Type | Hourly Rate | BMS Rate Type |
|------|--------------|------------:|---------------|
| 08:00 – 17:00 | ISPG | **$218.00/hr** less **10%** ($21.80) = **$196.20/hr** | ISRT |
| 08:00 – 17:00 | BEV | **$218.00/hr** | ISRT |
| 08:00 – 17:00 | Expanded Service | **$218.00/hr** less **15%** ($32.70) = **$185.30/hr** | SRT ES Only |

---

# Service Categories

## ISPG (In-Shop Power Generation)

- Base hourly rate: **$218.00/hr**
- Customer receives a **10% discount**
- Final billed rate: **$196.20/hr**
- Uses the standard **ISRT** BMS rate.

---

## BEV

- Standard hourly rate: **$218.00/hr**
- Uses the standard **ISRT** BMS rate.
- No discount applies.

---

## Expanded Service (ES)

- Base hourly rate: **$218.00/hr**
- Customer receives a **15% discount**
- Final billed rate: **$185.30/hr**
- Applies to **SRT ES** work only.

---

# Coordinator Responsibilities

Before preparing a quotation or work order:

- Determine whether the work is ISPG, BEV, or Expanded Service.
- Apply the appropriate customer discount where applicable.
- Verify the correct BMS labor type.
- Confirm that Expanded Service rates are only used for **SRT ES** work.

---

# Common Mistakes

## Incorrect Discount

Verify whether the service qualifies for:

- 10% ISPG discount
- 15% Expanded Service discount

Do not apply discounts to BEV work.

---

## Incorrect BMS Rate Type

Always verify the appropriate BMS labor type before invoicing.

- ISPG → ISRT
- BEV → ISRT
- Expanded Service → SRT ES Only

---

## Incorrect Service Classification

Ensure the work order has been classified correctly before selecting the hourly rate.
