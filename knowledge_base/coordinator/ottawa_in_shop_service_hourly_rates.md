## Purpose

This document provides the standard hourly billing rates for Ottawa In-Shop Generator, BEV, and Expanded Service work.

It is intended to help Service Coordinators determine the appropriate labor rate and BMS rate type when preparing quotations and work orders for in-shop service.

> **Note**
>
> This document is for **internal use only**.
>
> Rates may change without notice.

# Effective Date

- Effective: **January 1, 2026**

# Standard Business Hours

## Monday to Friday (Hours of Operation)

| Time | Service Type | Hourly Rate | BMS Rate Type |
|------|--------------|------------:|---------------|
| 07:00 – 15:30 | ISPG | **$201.00/hr** less **10%** ($20.00) = **$181.00/hr** | ISRT + Misc Rebate |
| 07:00 – 15:30 | BEV | **$201.00/hr** | ISRT |
| 07:00 – 15:30 | Expanded Service | **$201.00/hr** less **15%** ($30.15) = **$170.85/hr** | SRT ES Only |

---

# Service Categories

## ISPG (In-Shop Power Generation)

- Base hourly rate: **$201.00/hr**
- Customer receives a **10% discount**
- Final billed rate: **$181.00/hr**
- Uses **ISRT + Misc Rebate**.

---

## BEV

- Standard hourly rate: **$201.00/hr**
- Uses the standard **ISRT** BMS labor type.
- No discount applies.

---

## Expanded Service (ES)

- Base hourly rate: **$201.00/hr**
- Customer receives a **15% discount**
- Final billed rate: **$170.85/hr**
- Applies to **SRT ES** work only.

---

# Coordinator Responsibilities

Before preparing a quotation or work order:

- Determine whether the work is ISPG, BEV, or Expanded Service.
- Apply the correct customer discount where applicable.
- Select the appropriate BMS labor type.
- Verify that Expanded Service rates are only used for **SRT ES** work.

---

# Common Mistakes

## Incorrect Discount

Verify whether the service qualifies for:

- 10% ISPG discount
- 15% Expanded Service discount

Do not apply discounts to BEV work.

---

## Incorrect BMS Rate Type

Always verify the appropriate BMS labor type before invoicing.

- ISPG → ISRT + Misc Rebate
- BEV → ISRT
- Expanded Service → SRT ES Only

---

## Incorrect Service Classification

Ensure the work order has been classified correctly before selecting the hourly rate.
