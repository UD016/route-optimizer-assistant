## Purpose

This procedure explains how to add or remove favorite menu items in BMS.

Using Favorites allows Service Coordinators to quickly access frequently used forms and improve navigation efficiency.

---

## Opening the Favorites Menu

Locate the **yellow smiley face** icon on the BMS toolbar.

Click the icon to open the **Favorites** window.

---

## Adding or Removing Favorites

Within the Favorites window:

1. Locate the **Object Name** field.
2. Click the drop-down arrow beside **Object Name**.
3. Select the BMS form you wish to add or remove from your Favorites list.
4. Repeat as necessary for additional forms.

---

## Saving Changes

After completing your selections:

1. Save the record.

The Favorites menu will be updated immediately.

---

## Best Practices

Frequently used forms that are commonly added to Favorites include:

- Work Order
- Service Monitor
- Service Lookup
- Active Labor
- Order Entry
- Order Lookup
- Inquiry
- Customers
- Contacts
- Units
- Projects
- Contracts

Customize the Favorites list based on your daily responsibilities.

---

## Common Mistakes

### Changes Not Saved

Always save the record after adding or removing Favorites.

Unsaved changes will not be retained.

---

### Too Many Favorites

Avoid adding every available form.

Keeping only frequently used forms in your Favorites list makes navigation faster and easier.
