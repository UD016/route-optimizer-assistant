## Purpose

This policy defines the maximum working hours, driving limits, approval requirements, and scheduling guidelines for technicians.

The objective is to ensure employee safety, regulatory compliance, and consistent scheduling practices.

---

# In-Shop Technicians

## Standard Guidelines

Unless otherwise approved:

- Maximum **56 hours per week**
- Maximum **10 working hours per day**
- Maximum **6 consecutive working days**
- Rotating shift patterns should be limited whenever possible.
- Conduct a risk assessment whenever an exception is considered.

## Level 1 Exception

Requires **Plant Manager or Branch Manager approval**.

Permitted limits:

- Maximum **66 hours per week**
- Maximum **12 working hours per day**
- Maximum **3 days per week** at **12 working hours per day**

## Level 2 Exception

Requires approval from:

- BU Leadership
- Plant Manager
- Branch Manager

Required for:

- Any schedule exceeding the Level 1 limits.

# Field Service & Emergency Repairs

## Standard Guidelines

Unless otherwise approved:

- Maximum **56 hours per week**
- Maximum **12 working hours per day**
- Maximum **6 consecutive working days**
- Conduct a risk assessment whenever an exception is considered.

## Level 1 Exception

Requires **Branch Manager approval**.

Permitted limits:

- Maximum **72 hours per week**
- Maximum **3 days per week** at **14 working hours per day**
- Maximum **240 hours per month**
- Minimum **2 days of rest every two-week period**

## Level 2 Exception

Requires approval from:

- BU Leadership
- Plant Manager

Required for:

- Any schedule exceeding the Level 1 limits.

---

# Driving Limits

These limits apply whenever technicians are operating company vehicles.

## Standard Guidelines

Unless otherwise approved:

- Maximum **10 hours driving per 24-hour period**
- Minimum **15-minute break every 2 hours of driving**
- Combined driving time and working time must not exceed **12 hours**
- Travel from the place of rest to the work site is included in the calculation.

## Level 1 Exception

Requires **Branch Manager approval**.

Permitted limit:

- Maximum **14 hours combined driving and working time**

## Level 2 Exception

Requires approval from:

- BU Leadership

Required for:

- Any schedule exceeding the Level 1 limits.

## Branch Manager Authority

The Branch Manager may approve schedules up to the Level 1 limits only.

Any request exceeding those limits must follow the Level 2 approval process.

---

# Coordinator Responsibilities

When scheduling technicians:

- Verify that scheduled hours remain within policy limits.
- Consider total weekly hours before assigning additional work.
- Consider cumulative driving time.
- Ensure required breaks are respected.
- Do not exceed approval limits without obtaining the appropriate authorization.
- Perform a risk assessment whenever an exception is requested.
